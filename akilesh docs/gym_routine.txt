6-Day Muscle Building & Fat Loss Split 
Schedule

Monday: Upper Body A

Tuesday: Lower Body A

Wednesday: Zone 2 Cardio + Core + Mobility

Thursday: Upper Body B

Friday: Lower Body B

Saturday: Optional Zone 2 Cardio + Mobility

Sunday: Rest or Light Walk
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================================================================================================
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DAY 1 – UPPER BODY A (Chest & Back Focus)

Barbell Bench Press – 3×8–10

Pull-Ups (Assisted if needed) – 3×6–10

Incline Dumbbell Bench Press – 3×8–10

Seated Cable Row – 3×8–12

Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 3×12–15
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================================================================================================
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DAY 2 – LOWER BODY A (Quad-Dominant)

Back Squat – 4×6–8

Romanian Deadlift – 3×8–10

Walking Lunges – 2×10–12 each leg

Leg Press – 3×10–12

Plank – 3×30–45 sec
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================================================================================================
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DAY 3 – CARDIO

Cardio (30–40 min): Treadmill incline walk or stationary bike

Core: Hanging Leg Raises – 3×12–15 + Side Planks – 2×30 sec each side
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================================================================================================
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DAY 4 – UPPER BODY B (Shoulders & Arms Focus)

Overhead Barbell Press – 3×6–8

Barbell Rows (Pendlay or Bent-Over) – 3×8–10

Dumbbell Chest Fly (Flat or Incline) – 2×12–15

Face Pulls – 3×12–15

Superset: Barbell Curl + Triceps Rope Pushdown – 3×10–12 each
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================================================================================================
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DAY 5 – LOWER BODY B (Hamstring/Glute-Dominant)

Deadlift (Conventional or Sumo) – 4×5–6

Front Squat (or Goblet Squat if no barbell) – 3×8–10

Hip Thrust (Barbell) – 3×8–12

Standing Calf Raises – 4×12–15

Cable Crunch – 3×12–15
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================================================================================================
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DAY 6 –CARDIO 

Cardio (30–40 min): treadmill

Mobility: Hip flexor stretch, hamstring stretch, cat-cow, ankle mobility
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
============================================================================================================================================
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DAY 7 – REST OR LIGHT WALK
